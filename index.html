Book Summary

"Retrograde" is a book that contains the life hacking formula of Otaku Peasants who realized an automatic monthly income of 100 million won. They completed their own 'Retrograde 7-step' theory through trial and error. This book not only discusses theories on making money but also provides concrete methods.
